## ðŸª– Why Police Are Targeted Early

1. **Control of force**
2. **Control of enforcement discretion**
3. **Erosion of neutral legitimacy**

Policing institutions provide all three.

Early capture allows movements to:
- neutralise internal resistance,
- shape enforcement priorities,
- and condition the public to accept coercion as normal or necessary.

Without policing capture, authoritarian power remains fragile.
