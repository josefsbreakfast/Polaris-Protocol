## What makes PREVENT an authoritarian seedling

PREVENT introduces key elements of such systems:
- pre-crime logic
- anticipatory suspicion
- monitoring of affect and behaviour
- normalisation of reporting others
- low evidentiary thresholds
- escalation pathways that are difficult to reverse  

These features replicate structural components of authoritarian governance even when embedded in a democratic setting.

## How subjective interpretation expands institutional power
Because PREVENT relies on human judgement, institutions gain broad discretion over what counts as risk.

This conditioning is not imposed through force but through fear of misinterpretation, creating a population that behaves in a more controlled, less expressive manner.
