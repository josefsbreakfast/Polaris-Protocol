> "This cluster uses Elbit as a bridge-point case study to examine how defence technology, legal compliance, protest pressure, public-order governance, diaspora visibility, AI risk modelling, and atrocity-prevention obligations become entangled under prolonged moral emergency. It asks religious scholars and grounded communities not for political recruitment, but for counsel: how to preserve human obligation, lawful judgment, and de-escalatory wisdom when institutions begin treating moral crisis as a risk-management problem."
