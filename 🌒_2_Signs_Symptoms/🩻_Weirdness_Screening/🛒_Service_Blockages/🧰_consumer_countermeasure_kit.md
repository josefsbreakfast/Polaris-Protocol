**First created:** 2025-11-02 | **Last updated:** 2026-05-19  
*Practical steps to document, route around, and escalate blockages.*

---

## 🧭 Orientation
A living checklist for survivors encountering retail denial. Prioritise **evidence capture** and **safe alternates**.

---

## ✅ Quick Checklist
- Screenshot listing, cart, error (with timestamps visible).  
- Save page HTML/PDF if possible; copy error codes and correlation IDs.  
- Try alternates: variant SKU, different retailer, different network (mobile vs home), signed‑out vs signed‑in.  
- Record contextual factors (news, protests, policy dates).

## 📨 Escalation Templates (stubs)
- **Vendor support request** — request auth IDs, decline reason, feature flag status.  
- **Issuer inquiry** — confirm issuer‑side vs merchant‑side denial.  
- **Regulator note** — log pattern with evidence bundle (ICO/Trading Standards/PSR).
