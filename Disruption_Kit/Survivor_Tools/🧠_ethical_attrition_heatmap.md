## ğŸ§  Attrition as Signal, Not Turnover  

Healthy organisations experience turnover that is:
- distributed,
- role-balanced,
- and knowledge-replenished.

Ethical attrition looks different:
- departures are clustered,
- replacements are less experienced or more rigid,
- remaining staff carry unsustainable moral load.

When ethical attrition is misread as normal turnover, institutions miss their **last early warning**.
