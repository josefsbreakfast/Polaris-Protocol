Regarding DPA and subject access requests, in relation to duties under article 15:  

- 15(3) → 📄 “Show me the data” (disclosure)
- 15(1)(a–h) → ⚙️ “Explain the processing”
- 15(1) opening line → ✅ “Confirm whether processing exists”  

A lot of you are doing this:  
- providing generic processing descriptions  
- but avoiding giving the actual data copy (15(3))  

That’s non-compliant.  

These are the rights the data subject can access, and should be given without undue obstruction:  
- Disclosure of data → Article 15(3).
- Processing information → Article 15(1)(a–h)
- Confirmation of processing → Article 15(1) opening clause

You can't just shove policies at people when they are identifying a potential data breach. It is your responsibility to make reasonable assurances.
