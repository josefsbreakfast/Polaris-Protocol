## 🛰️ Orientation  

**Political Dramaturgy** examines governance as performance.

This cluster studies:
- political aesthetics,
- archetypes,
- symbolic authority,
- charisma,
- public ritual,
- emotional staging,
- and the theatrical dimensions of legitimacy.

It focuses on the ways institutions and leaders become narratively legible through:
- vibe,
- costume,
- symbolism,
- emotional tonality,
- and performative coherence.

This includes both democratic and authoritarian systems.

The question is not merely:
> “Who has power?”

But:
> “What kind of story does power believe it is inside?”
