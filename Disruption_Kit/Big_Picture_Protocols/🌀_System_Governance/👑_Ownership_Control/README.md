# 👑 Ownership & Control
**First created:** 2025-10-13 | **Last updated:** 2026-05-08  
*Where remit fractures, custody diffuses, and operational power actually determines outcomes.*

---

## 🛰️ Orientation

**Ownership & Control** maps what happens after law, policy, and procurement collide in practice.

Where:

- 🌀 *Systems & Governance* describes oversight architecture,  
- ⚖️ *Legal & State Governance* analyses statutory and constitutional design,  

this cluster asks the operational question:

> **Who actually controls the process once rules meet reality?**

Here we track:

- custody gaps,
- mandate misfit,
- escalation vacuums,
- bottleneck authority,
- insulation through indispensability,
- and outcome ownership when harm emerges through delay rather than declaration.

This is where governance becomes concrete.

---

## 🧭 What This Cluster Tracks

- **Process Custody** — who signs, who stalls, who has stop authority.
- **Mandate Misfit** — when no institution’s primary output matches the harm.
- **Bottleneck Power** — choke points as operational leverage.
- **Custodial Capture** — when embedded actors become too useful to remove.
- **Ownership Diffusion** — responsibility fragmented across actors.
- **Outcome Ownership** — who bears consequence when systems “merely delay.”

This cluster is structural, not rhetorical.
