- whether consequences follow status

- quiet resignations

From a deterrence perspective, these outcomes are indistinguishable from non-action.

- resolved through parallel systems

- unreadable  
- technical

- harsh potential penalties  
- theoretical maximum sentences

- certainty matters more than severity  
- frequency matters more than extremity  
- visibility matters more than symbolism  

An unseen punishment does not alter behaviour.

## üèõÔ∏è Visibility and the Rule of Law

- process

People then self-govern based on rumour and power cues.

## üß® The Institutional Fear of Visibility

- sets precedent  
- limits discretion

- public distrust  
- conspiracy narratives  
- democratic fragility
