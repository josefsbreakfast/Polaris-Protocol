## The Ent Problem

Some institutions are not captured.  
They are not malicious.  
They are not even especially incompetent.

They are **slow**.

They deliberate.
They consult.
They commission reviews.
They request further evidence.
They await consensus.
They schedule the next meeting.

Years pass.

The world changes.
Infrastructure embeds.
Harms accumulate.

Eventually, a decision is reached â€” about a system that has already become unavoidable.

These institutions are Ents.

## Why Ents Are Not Villains

Ents are:
- principled
- process-oriented
- historically grounded
- deeply cautious
- allergic to rash action

They value:
- precedent
- proportionality
- legitimacy
- and being *seen* to act correctly

In isolation, these are virtues.

In fast-moving technological and securitised environments, they become liabilities.
