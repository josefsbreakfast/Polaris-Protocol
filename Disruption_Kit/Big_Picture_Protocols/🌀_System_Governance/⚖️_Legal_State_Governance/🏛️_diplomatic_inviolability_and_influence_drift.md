- inviolable under diplomatic law,

- friction feels like betrayal,
- delay feels responsible.

## ğŸ” Drift Mechanics (How It Happens)
Influence drift typically follows this pattern:

1. Early crisis framed as exceptional.
2. Private reassurance substitutes for public boundary-setting.
3. Reviews are initiated, not suspensions.
4. Time passes under continuity.
5. Facts on the ground change irreversibly.
6. Later adjustment is framed as pragmatism.

No single step looks decisive.  
Together they entrench posture.
