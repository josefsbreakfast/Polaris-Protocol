**First created:** 2025-11-20 | **Last updated:** 2026-05-09

## 🛰️ Orientation  
States compete through **aesthetics** more than policy — through glamour, spectacle, emotional resonance, memeability, and vibe export.

## ✨ Key Features

## 🧿 Analysis / Content  
### 1. Aesthetic Capital  
A nation’s cultural output generates desire and attachment — K-pop, anime, carnival, haute couture.

### 2. Emotional Markets  
People adopt nations like fandoms; emotional resonance is currency.

### 3. Aesthetic Deficits  
Bunker states with no charisma lose global influence regardless of GDP or force.

### 4. Aesthetic Competition  
Nations compete to be lovable, compelling, memeable — the emotionally magnetic ones win.
