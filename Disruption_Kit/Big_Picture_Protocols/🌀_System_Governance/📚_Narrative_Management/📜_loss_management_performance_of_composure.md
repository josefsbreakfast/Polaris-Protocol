# 📜 Loss Management and the Performance of Composure  
**First created:** 2025-11-12 | **Last updated:** 2026-05-09  
*The 'heap of all your winnings' stanza as blueprint for reputational recovery.*  

---

## 🛰️ Orientation  
When Kipling writes 'If you can make one heap of all your winnings…' he’s describing crisis theatre: how to lose everything, start again, and never let it show.  

---

## ✨ Key Features  
- **Containment as Performance:** composure equals credibility.
- **Silence as Survival:** never breathe a word about your loss — avoid feeding the narrative loop.
- **Reconstruction as Identity:** rebuilding quietly creates authority through endurance.

---

## 🧿 Analysis  
This is the template for institutional recovery messaging. Modern PR and crisis-communications handbooks trace the same rhythm: acknowledge privately, reframe publicly, carry on visibly.
