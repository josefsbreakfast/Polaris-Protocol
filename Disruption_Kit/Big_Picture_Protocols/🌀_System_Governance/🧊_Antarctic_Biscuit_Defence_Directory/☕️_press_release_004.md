# 🛰️ Orientation  

*Biscuit Press: Release 004* focuses on institutional spin under conditions where:
- public credibility has already begun collapsing,
- operational secrecy is failing,
- and internal incidents are becoming impossible to convincingly deny.

Structured as a formal clarification statement, the release satirises:
- public relations containment,
- bureaucratic euphemism,
- institutional reputation management,
- and the tendency of organisations to retroactively redefine disasters as intentional exercises.

The humour emerges from the widening contradiction between:
- visibly absurd events,
- and the increasingly polished language used to explain them.

The piece particularly targets the logic of:
> “nothing went wrong because everything that happened was actually part of the plan.”

---

# 📰 Press Release  

The Antarctic Biscuit Defence Directorate (ABDD) wishes to clarify recent events that have circulated in public discourse.  

---

## 📸 Social Media Photos  

- Images of biscuits posted on Instagram by ABDD personnel were not operational disclosures.  

- These biscuits were shared in a *personal capacity* and should not be mistaken for official policy.  

- We remind the public that hashtags such as:
  > #CustardCreamOps
  were purely morale-building exercises.  

---

## 🐜 Community Picnic Exercise  

- Reports of agents being hospitalised after encountering “fire ants” during a biscuit-eating excursion are exaggerated.  

- The activity was a planned *community engagement drill* designed to test resilience under unexpected conditions.  

- While several participants required precautionary medical attention, at no point was biscuit sovereignty at risk.  

---

The ABDD remains fully committed to:
- transparency,
- snack security,
- and safe dunking practices in all theatres of operation.  

*— End Release —*
