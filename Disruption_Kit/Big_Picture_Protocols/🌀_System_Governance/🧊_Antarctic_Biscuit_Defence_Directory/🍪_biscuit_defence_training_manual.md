# 🍪 Biscuit Defence Training Manual  
**First created:** 2025-09-30 | **Last updated:** 2026-05-14  
*A simplified operational handbook for Antarctic Biscuit Defence Directorate field personnel, illustrating how bureaucratic systems collapse ambiguity into rigid categories, procedural suspicion, and absurd behavioural enforcement.*  

---

## 🛰️ Orientation  

*🍪 Biscuit Defence Training Manual* satirises:
- binary institutional thinking,
- low-resolution threat modelling,
- workplace compliance culture,
- and the bureaucratic tendency to simplify complex human behaviour into administratively manageable categories.

Presented as an internal training document for ABDD field agents, the node exaggerates the logic of systems that:
- divide populations into rigid classifications,
- discourage nuance,
- reward suspicion,
- and operationalise trivial behavioural markers into indicators of loyalty or threat.

The humour emerges from the escalation of:
- everyday biscuit preferences
into:
- security doctrine,
- behavioural monitoring,
- and quasi-military enforcement logic.

While comedic in tone, the structure mirrors recognisable patterns found in:
- institutional training culture,
- ideological simplification,
- algorithmic categorisation,
- and administrative systems that prioritise procedural clarity over human complexity.

---

## 📑 Overview  

You are either a:
- **Biscuit Stealer**
or:
- **Biscuit Loyalist**.  

There are:
- no partial classifications,
- no “maybes,”
- and no “50% biscuit stealers.”  

Operational certainty is mandatory.  

---

## 📝 Spotting A Biscuit Stealer  

Indicators may include:  

- Carrying biscuits not listed on the Approved Custard Cream Register.  

- Refusing to dunk at company-approved angles.  

- Mentioning bread more than twice in conversation.  

- Appearing suspicious in general  
  (*agent discretion encouraged*).  

---

## ✅ Approved Agent Responses  

### 🛒 Redirect Suspect  

- “Kindly visit the custard cream aisle.”  

- Deploy GPS rerouting if necessary.  

---

### 📲 Wellness Reminder  

Send daily motivational notifications including:  

> “Hobnobs make you happy.”  

---

### 🥚 Egg Deployment (Last Resort)  

- Authorised only when gentle nudges fail.  

- Agents are reminded to distinguish between:
  - hostile actors,
  - and ordinary breakfast ingredients.  

---

## 🚫 Prohibited Behaviour  

Agents must not:  

- attempt nuance,  

- question the binary classification framework,  

- ask whether individuals may be “partly” biscuit-stealers,  

- or dunk biscuits outside authorised protocol  
  (*disciplinary tea procedures may apply*).
