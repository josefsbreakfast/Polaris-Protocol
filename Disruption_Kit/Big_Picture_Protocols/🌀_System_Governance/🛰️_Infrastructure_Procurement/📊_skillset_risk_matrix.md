### 3. Key Governance Insights  

#### **a. Technical Skill Is Not the Main Risk Vector**

- interpretive authority, and  
- multi-agency visibility  

than with deep technical skill.

Environmental sabotage exploits **interpretation**, not infrastructure.

#### **b. Duty Increases Faster Than Capability**  
In several roles (notably safeguarding, Prevent, public law, healthcare governance),  
**recognition duty exceeds technical literacy**.

When judgement fails, harm scales rapidly.

If misclassification is not recognised here, it propagates outward and hardens.
