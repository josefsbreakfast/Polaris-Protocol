## 3. Digital Literacy as Infrastructure, Not Expertise  

Digital literacy does **not** mean:
- everyone learns to code  
- everyone understands systems engineering  
- everyone becomes a technical specialist  

It means:
- people understand what systems *do*  
- people recognise where errors and bias can occur  
- people know when outputs require verification  
- people feel entitled to question digital authority  

Like roads, literacy only works if **enough people share it**.

This is why it functions as infrastructure, not enrichment.
