### 4. What Makes a Floating Commons Different  

A **Floating Commons** is defined not by where it floats, but by how it governs.

Minimum design principles:
- **Informed consent** to participation  
- **Real exit pathways** without penalty  
- **Shared rule-making authority**  
- **Transparent decision processes**  
- **External legal interface** (no total autonomy claims)  
- **Labour protections and remuneration**  
- **Environmental responsibility**  

If any of these are missing, it is not a commons.  
It is an enclave.
