Cross-linking accelerates this by:

- making analogies legible
- allowing partial ideas to meet complementary ones

The link is not secondary.

### 5. The Ethics of Connection

Not all networking is benign.

Connection must **increase agency on both sides**, or it becomes another containment vector.
