- Science  
- Technology  
- Engineering  
- Mathematics  
- Finance-adjacent systems (including cryptocurrency ecosystems)  

These are not neutral domains.

They are **legitimacy engines** — environments where capital, intelligence, and authority converge and reinforce one another.

When regression occurs here, it does not remain local.  
It propagates outward as **norm-setting behaviour**.

A recurring narrative within these systems is that current shifts represent a **return to a more natural order**.

What is being pursued is a **retroactive construction**:

- A stable hierarchy  
- A compliant social structure  
- A frictionless expression of power  

Such a system did not exist in the coherent form being imagined.

> It is not a return. It is a reconstruction.
