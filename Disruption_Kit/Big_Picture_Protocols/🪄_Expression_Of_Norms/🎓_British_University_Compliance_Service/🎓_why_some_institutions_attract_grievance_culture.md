### 6. Why Harm Clusters, Not Spreads  

Institutions most prone to grievance culture tend to share:
- prestige scarcity  
- opaque advancement criteria  
- high competition for limited rewards  
- weak pastoral containment  
- performative rather than structural wellbeing  

By contrast, environments that:
- normalise differentiated excellence  
- allow lateral success  
- de-pathologise non-linear trajectories  

produce **far fewer grievance explosions**, because individuals have somewhere to *land* when the ladder stops fitting.

Grievance culture emerges when:
