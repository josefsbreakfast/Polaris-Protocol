## 🛰️ Overview  

“May the Force be with you” originates as a meaningful in-universe blessing from *Star Wars* (1977), created by George Lucas.  

On May 4, the phrase mutates into:  
**“May the Fourth be with you.”**

This phonetic coincidence produces a yearly internet-wide ritual now known as *Star Wars Day*.  

What matters is not the joke itself, but what it reveals:  

> **The internet uses dates as triggers for mass behavioural alignment.**

## 📅 Calendar as Protocol  

Certain dates function as **low-friction coordination points**:

- May 4 → Star Wars posting ritual  
- April 20 (4/20) → cannabis culture signal  
- Other anniversaries → fandom, political, or subcultural activation  

These dates require:
- no organiser  
- no instruction  
- no originality  

Only **recognition + participation**.
