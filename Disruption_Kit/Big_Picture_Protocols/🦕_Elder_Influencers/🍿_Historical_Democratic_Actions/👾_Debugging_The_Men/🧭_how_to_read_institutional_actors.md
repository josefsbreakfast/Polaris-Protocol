# 1. The Four Archetypes  
Institutional actors fall roughly into four categories when harm is disclosed:

### **1. The Knowing**  
They understand exactly what is happening.  
They may have known for years.  
They view the survivor as a threat to stability.

### **2. The Pretending**  
They suspect the truth but avoid confirming it.  
They protect themselves through performative ignorance.

### **3. The Blind**  
They genuinely do not see the pattern.  
They lack experience, context, or trauma literacy.

### **4. The Trapped**  
They see the truth but cannot act.  
They fear retaliation, job loss, collapse, or exposure.

You can read institutions by identifying these four positions.
