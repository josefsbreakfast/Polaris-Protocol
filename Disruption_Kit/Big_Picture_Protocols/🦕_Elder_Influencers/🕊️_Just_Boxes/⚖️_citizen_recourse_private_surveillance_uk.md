## ðŸ§© 4. Practical Steps for Individuals  

1. **Document** events with timestamps, screenshots, and metadata (hash if possible).  
2. **File Subject Access Requests (SARs)** to discover who holds your data.  
3. **Submit ICO complaint**, then escalate to civil action if warranted.  
4. **Engage MPs or All-Party Parliamentary Groups** on data accountability for public visibility.  
5. **Network with journalists or digital-rights groups** for corroboration and safety.  

Even partial evidence can trigger regulatory attention and establish public record.

## ðŸ§© 5. Reform Outlook

- Strengthened **whistle-blower protections** within intelligence and tech sectors.
