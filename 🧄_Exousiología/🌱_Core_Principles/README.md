# 🌱 Core Principles  
**First created:** 2026-05-05 | **Last updated:** 2026-05-05  
*Working commitments grounding how authority is understood, evaluated, and challenged within Exousiología.*

---

## 🧭 Purpose  

This folder defines the **core working principles** of 🧄 Exousiología.  
These are not fixed doctrines or universal truths. They are **provisional commitments** used to orient analysis, guide diagnosis, and prevent drift into abstraction or domination.

They exist to answer a practical question:

> *On what basis do we recognise, accept, or refuse authority?*

---

## 🌱 What Lives Here  

The nodes in this folder establish the conceptual ground for the system, including:

- what authority is and is not  
- how legitimacy is formed and lost  
- how power takes shape and operates  
- where authority begins, ends, and fails  
- how authority becomes visible, recognised, or contested  

Each principle is:
- **testable against lived reality**  
- **open to revision under pressure**  
- **grounded in embodiment, not abstraction**  

---

## ⚖️ How to Use This Folder  

These files are not designed to be read once and accepted. They are meant to be:

- **returned to during analysis**  
- **used as diagnostic anchors**  
- **challenged when they fail to explain reality**  

If a principle cannot account for:
- harm  
- breakdown  
- or lived contradiction  

then it must be revised or discarded.

## 🌿 Note on Language  

Where abstraction obscures meaning, ecological and embodied metaphors may be used to clarify:

- structure (roots, canopies, systems)  
- behaviour (predation, care, coordination)  
- change (succession, collapse, regrowth)  

These are not decorative—they are used to keep authority **legible, grounded, and accountable to reality**.
