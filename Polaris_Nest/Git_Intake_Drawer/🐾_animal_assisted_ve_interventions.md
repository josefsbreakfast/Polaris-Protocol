### 2. Where This Actually Sits in UK Structures

- youth trauma services  
- CAMHS pilots

- participant consent  
- ethics board approval  
- data minimisation  
- zero involvement of intelligence agencies  

There is **no CT mandate** allowing an officer to enter a home to condition a pet.
