## Diagnostic Questions  
To identify current governance mode, ask:

- Are decisions fast, slow, or stalled?  
- Is communication warm, neutral, or defensive?  
- Are staff improvising or ritualistic?  
- Does responsibility move forward or sideways?  
- Are people acting from fear, uncertainty, or stability?  

The mode explains the behaviour.
