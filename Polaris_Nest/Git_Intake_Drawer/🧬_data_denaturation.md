### 1. What denaturation actually means  

In protein physics:
- Primary sequence may remain intact  
- Secondary and tertiary structure collapse  
- Function is destroyed  
- Renaturation is rare and conditional  

The information still exists.  
The pathway back does not.

Data denaturation follows the same pattern.

### 2. The myth of data reversibility  

Data is often described as infinitely correctable:
- Fields can be edited  
- Records can be amended  
- Backups exist  

This confuses **bit-level mutability** with **system-level reversibility**.

Meaning in data systems is:
- Distributed  
- Contextual  
- Authority-dependent  
- Time-bound  

Once lost, it cannot simply be restored by changing a field.

Denatured data is cheaper to live with than to repair.
