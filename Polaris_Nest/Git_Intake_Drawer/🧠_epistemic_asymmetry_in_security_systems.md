## What Epistemic Asymmetry Means

Epistemic asymmetry exists when:
- one group operates with privileged knowledge
- others are governed by the outputs of that knowledge
- and the governed cannot access, verify, or contest the underlying assumptions

This is not secrecy in the legal sense.

It is **structural inequality of understanding**.

## Bias Without Bad Faith
