# 1. **Human Targeting vs Architectural Targeting**

### **Human Targeting (intentional)**  
- Someone wants to harm you.  
- They manipulate processes deliberately.  
- They coordinate others.  
- Escalations have strategy.  
- Behaviour is personalised.

### **Architectural Targeting (structural)**  
- No single actor intends harm.  
- Multiple systems independently misinterpret behaviour.  
- Metadata accumulates and self-confirms.  
- Behavioural drift is amplified across tools.  
- Escalation is procedural, not personal.

Both create the *experience* of being surrounded by hostile forces.  
But only one is driven by intent.
