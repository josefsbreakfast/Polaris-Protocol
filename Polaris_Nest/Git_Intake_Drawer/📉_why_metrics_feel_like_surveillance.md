### 3) The role of opacity

### 4) Scale distortion in small systems
In small projects, low absolute numbers mean:
- one crawler can dominate activity,
- one reader can account for most depth,
- and normal indexing looks anomalous.

Metrics designed for scale behave strangely at small size,

- targeted consistency,
- coordinated access patterns,
- repeated access to specific materials,
- and convergence across platforms.

Ordinary analytics lack these features.

Confusing the two inflates risk perception
and obscures real indicators when they matter.
