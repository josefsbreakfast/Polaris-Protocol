## Predictive logic vs predictive infrastructure
Predictive logic is the belief that behaviour today can indicate risk tomorrow. PREVENT gave universities:
- indicators lists
- vulnerability frameworks
- behavioural templates
- anticipatory guidance
Predictive infrastructure, by contrast, involves machine-learning models or risk-scoring algorithms. Universities did not adopt these; they adopted the mindset.

- expressions of anger
- political curiosity
- withdrawal or disengagement
- changes in dress or social circle
These are subjective and culturally loaded interpretations. They foster a predictive surveillance environment without centralised tech.
