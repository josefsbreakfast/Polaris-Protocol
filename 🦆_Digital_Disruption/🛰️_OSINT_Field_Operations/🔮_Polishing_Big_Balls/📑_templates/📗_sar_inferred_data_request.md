This template is intended for use where you believe your data may be processed by a public authority or contracted supplier.

## Template

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to make a Subject Access Request under applicable data protection legislation.

Please provide all personal data relating to me that you hold, including but not limited to:

- any inferred, derived, or predicted attributes,  
- profiles, risk indicators, flags, or segmentation labels,  
- metadata relating to how my data has been used, shared, or analysed,  
- records of automated or semi-automated processing, where applicable.

This request includes data processed directly, indirectly, or via analytic, behavioural, or profiling systems, whether held in structured or unstructured form.

If any data is withheld, please specify the category and the lawful basis relied upon.

Kind regards,  
[Name]  
[Identifying details as required]
