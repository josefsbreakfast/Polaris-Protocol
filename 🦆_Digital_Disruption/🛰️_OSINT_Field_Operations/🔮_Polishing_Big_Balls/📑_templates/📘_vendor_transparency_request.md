This template is for contacting private suppliers or contractors.

It does not assert a right to disclosure; it asks for clarification of role and materials supplied.

---

## Template

Dear [Organisation],

I am seeking to understand the governance arrangements for your services as delivered under public-sector contracts.

Could you please confirm whether, in relation to **[name of public authority / service]**, you supplied any of the following:

- DPIA templates or draft DPIAs,  
- risk assessments or data protection analyses,  
- documentation describing system risks, safeguards, or limitations.

If so, I would appreciate confirmation of the nature and date of those materials.

This request does not seek confidential client information, only clarification of your role in supporting data protection governance.

Kind regards,  
[Name]
