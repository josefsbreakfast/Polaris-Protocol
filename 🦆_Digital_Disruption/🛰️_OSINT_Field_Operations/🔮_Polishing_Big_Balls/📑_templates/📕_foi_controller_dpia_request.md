*A neutral request for governance documents relating to a specific data service.*

---

This template is intended for use with public authorities acting as **data controllers**.

It requests the existence and disclosure of DPIAs and related governance material, without assuming wrongdoing or demanding interpretation.

---

## Template

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to request information under the Freedom of Information Act.

Please provide copies of any Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIAs), risk assessments, or equivalent documents relating to the processing of personal data in connection with:

- the use of **[name of system / service / supplier]**,  
- for the purpose of **[brief description of function, e.g. analytics, triage, monitoring]**,  
- by or on behalf of **[name of public authority]**.

If disclosure of full content is exempt, please confirm:

- whether such documents exist,  
- their dates,  
- their authors or approving roles,  
- and the scope of processing they cover.

Please also provide any procurement or assurance documents that specifically reference data protection, profiling, inference, or risk in relation to this service.

This request is limited to recorded information held at the time of the request.

Kind regards,  
[Name]

---

*This request is intended to clarify governance, not to challenge decisions.*
