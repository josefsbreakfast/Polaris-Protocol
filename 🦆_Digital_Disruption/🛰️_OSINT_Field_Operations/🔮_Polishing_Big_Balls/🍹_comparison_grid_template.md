## How to read the grid

Look for:
- **consistency** across columns  
- **timing mismatches** (e.g. DPIA after deployment)  
- **scope drift** between â€œadvisoryâ€ and operational use

- repeated phrases across different organisations  
- claims that are difficult to reconcile with lived data  

You are comparing *descriptions*, not intentions.
