## Survivor Sovereignty

Survivor sovereignty means:
- survivors decide whether to speak,
- survivors decide what to share,
- survivors decide when to disengage.

No explanation is owed.
No disclosure is required.
No participation is assumed.

This project exists to **reduce pressure on survivors**, not redirect it.

---

## Survivors Are Not Archives

Survivors are not:
- documentation tools,
- narrative validators,
- or gap-fillers for institutional failure.

They are not responsible for:
- clarifying redactions,
- correcting public misunderstanding,
- or satisfying curiosity.

Demanding this labour reproduces harm.

---

## Listening as a Hard Constraint

When survivors state:
- boundaries,
- preferences,
- objections,
- or refusals,

those statements constrain:
- scope,
- tone,
- and direction of work.

Listening that does not change behaviour is not listening.
It is extraction.

---

## Silence and Withdrawal

Silence is not consent.
Silence is not ambiguity.
Silence is not absence.

Withdrawal is:
- valid,
- final,
- and not open to reinterpretation.

Attempts to read meaning into survivor silence are prohibited.

---

## Survivor Disagreement

Survivors do not form a monolith.

Disagreement among survivors does not:
- invalidate any individual,
- license selective listening,
- or justify override.

Where survivor perspectives diverge:
- the most restrictive position governs,
- and the least harmful path is chosen.

---

## Public Curiosity Versus Survivor Safety

Public interest does not override survivor safety.

This includes:
- speculation about identity,
- amplification of graphic material,
- and repetitive exposure to traumatic detail.

Information can be accurate and still be harmful.
Accuracy is not a sufficient ethical test.

---

## Representation and Amplification

This project does not:
- speak on behalf of survivors,
- summarise survivor experience,
- or present itself as representative.

Where survivor voices are amplified:
- consent must be explicit,
- context must be preserved,
- and withdrawal must remain possible.

Amplification is reversible.

---

## Temporal Respect

Survivor timelines do not align with:
- investigative cycles,
- media interest,
- or public urgency.

Pressure to ‚Äúact now‚Äù is not a survivor obligation.
Urgency belongs to institutions, not those harmed.

## Refusal of Trauma Porn

This work refuses:
- sensational framing,
- graphic emphasis,
- and narrative escalation for attention.

Exposure without care is harm.
Repetition without purpose is harm.

Trauma is not leverage.

---

## Accountability Without Extraction

Justice does not require survivor exhaustion.

Accountability pursued through:
- financial scrutiny,
- governance audit,
- and lawful process

reduces reliance on survivor testimony and re-exposure.

This is intentional.

---

## Closing

Listening is not passive.
It is an active refusal to cause further harm.

Survivor sovereignty is not optional.
It is the condition under which this work proceeds at all.
