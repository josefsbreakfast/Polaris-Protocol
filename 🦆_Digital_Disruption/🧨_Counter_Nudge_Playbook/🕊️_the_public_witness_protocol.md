## 1. What Public Witness Is  
Public witness is the collective effort to ensure:

- **the event is seen**,  
- **its stakes are understood**,  
- **its timeline is preserved**,  
- **its human reality is not abstracted**,  
- **its meaning is not overwritten by institutional framing.**

It is not persuasion; it is documentation.

## 4. The Components of a Public Witness Protocol  
An emergent protocol can be seen across cases of political suffering:

### **a. Continuity**  
Regular updates prevent disappearance.

### **b. Specificity**  
Concrete details counteract euphemistic collapse.

### **c. Interpretive Clarity**  
Explaining the broader context (e.g., pre-trial detention under exceptional powers) enables comprehension.

### **d. Cross-verification**  
Multiple sources reduce misinformation risk.

### **e. Embodied Grounding**  
Describing physical reality (hospitalisation, symptoms) anchors the narrative.
