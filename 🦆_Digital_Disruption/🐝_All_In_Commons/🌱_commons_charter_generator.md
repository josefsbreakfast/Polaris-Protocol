## ðŸ§© Core Design Principles  

### 1. **Living Commitments, Not Static Law**  
Charters generated through this framework:
- Require periodic renewal or reaffirmation  
- Contain built-in sunset clauses  
- Can be suspended for repair without collapsing the commons  

Governance is treated as *care infrastructure*, not control architecture.

### 2. **Legibility Without Capture**  
The generator balances:
- **External legibility** (law, contracts, funders, regulators)  
- **Internal coherence** (shared norms, care, emotional reality)  

Legal compatibility is provided *without* surrendering narrative or moral sovereignty.

### 3. **Antifascist Accountability by Design**  
Every generated charter embeds:
- Explicit anti-capture clauses  
- Distributed authority  
- Named obligations to protect marginalised participants  

Power concentration is treated as a **failure mode**, not an edge case.
