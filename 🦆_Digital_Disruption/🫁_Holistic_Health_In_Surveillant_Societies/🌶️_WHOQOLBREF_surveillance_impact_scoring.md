This node proposes **WHOQOL-BREF** as a **mandatory impact and value-for-money scoring instrument** for any non-emergency surveillance or behavioural intervention applied to civilians.

The objective is not attribution, disclosure, or intelligence evaluation.

The objective is **human impact accounting**:
- Is quality of life improved, unchanged, or degraded?
- Is vulnerability reduced or amplified?
- Is public money being spent where it delivers the greatest protective return?

If an intervention cannot tolerate transparent quality-of-life measurement, it is **not safe for population-scale deployment**.
