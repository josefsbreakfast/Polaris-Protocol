## 🧭 Overview

This space documents the transition from **digital disruption** toward **rehabilitated technology**, culminating in the practice of **opening the source**.

It is both:

* a **project index** (what is being built, tested, and released), and
* a **philosophical layer** (why openness is necessary after systemic failure).

The guiding premise is simple:
closed systems concentrate power; open systems redistribute agency.

## 🧠 Core Philosophy

**1. Openness is not aesthetic — it is structural**
Open source is treated here as a **power rebalancing mechanism**, not a branding choice.

**2. Rehabilitation comes before release**
Broken systems made public without repair simply scale harm.

**3. Documentation is infrastructure**
Clarity, traceability, and reproducibility are treated as core outputs — not afterthoughts.

**4. Survivorship informs design**
Systems are evaluated based on how they behave under stress, misuse, and asymmetry.
