---

## 🧭 Purpose  

Disruption Kit occupies the middle operational layer of the protocol.

This folder focuses on:
- practical navigation,
- pattern recognition,
- survivability under pressure,
- and rebuilding agency incrementally over time.

The emphasis is:
- actionable understanding,
- medium-bandwidth practical guidance,
- and disruption-aware navigation rather than emergency triage.

---

## 📦 Typical Inclusions  

Files here may include:

- communication sovereignty guides,
- disruption-pattern analysis,
- pacing systems,
- anti-coercion frameworks,
- boundary-setting tools,
- refusal scripts,
- social or institutional disruption mapping,
- practical resilience strategies,
- narrative disruption methods,
- and non-digital containment navigation.

This folder may also contain:
- applied systems interpretation,
- practical countermeasures,
- and survivability-oriented pattern analysis.

---

## 🚫 Exclusions  

This folder is **not** for:
- immediate emergency triage,
- purely emotional grounding tools,
- highly abstract governance theory,
- archival research repositories,
- or deep strategic infrastructure planning.

Those belong elsewhere:
- 🌓 STAT Survivor Tools,
- 🌕 Resource Gathering,
- or higher-level systems clusters.

Ask:
