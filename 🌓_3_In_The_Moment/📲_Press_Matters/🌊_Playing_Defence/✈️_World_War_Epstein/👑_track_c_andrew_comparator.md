## ⚖️ Formalisation And Risk  

Andrew’s arrest, like Mandelson’s, also shows the value and limits of legal formalisation.

Formal process can stabilise risk by moving it into procedure.

But it can also create new pressure:

| Formal legal step | Stabilising effect | Destabilising effect |
|---|---|---|
| Police assessment | Creates official lane | Confirms seriousness |
| Arrest | Signals law applies | Produces institutional shock |
| Release without charge | Avoids immediate escalation | Leaves uncertainty unresolved |
| Document-release support | Signals transparency | Opens new exposure windows |
| Search / follow-up inquiry | Preserves evidence | Extends news cycle |

This is why the UK may look both exposed and more legally contained than other actors.

Formal process can be ugly.

But informal elite-risk management can be worse.
