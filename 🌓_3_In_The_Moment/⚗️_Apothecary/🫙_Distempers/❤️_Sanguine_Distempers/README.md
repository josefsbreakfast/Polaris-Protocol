# ❤️ Sanguine Distempers  
**First created:** 2026-05-21 | **Last updated:** 2026-05-21  
*Compulsive stimulation states associated with performativity, novelty dependency, visibility seeking, and perpetual engagement cycles.*  

---

## 🛰️ Orientation  

Sanguine distempers emerge when novelty-seeking, socially adaptive, or expressive tendencies become captured by systems optimised for perpetual stimulation and visibility reinforcement.  

These states are often characterised by:

- compulsive posting,
- engagement dependency,
- identity performance exhaustion,
- novelty fixation,
- and difficulty tolerating stillness or invisibility.  

Unlike ordinary sociability, creativity, or memetic play, sanguine distempers involve the gradual replacement of reflective agency with continuous responsiveness to platform rhythms and audience feedback systems.  

The informational environment becomes experienced as:

- permanently active,
- socially evaluative,
- emotionally stimulating,
- and visibility-dependent.  

Platform architectures frequently intensify these conditions through:

- metrics,
- virality incentives,
- social comparison,
- novelty prioritisation,
- and endless engagement loops.

## 🌊 Common Dynamics  

Sanguine distempers often emerge from adaptive capacities such as:

- humour,
- creativity,
- social responsiveness,
- improvisation,
- memetic fluency,
- and community participation.  

Destabilisation occurs when algorithmic systems continuously reward:

- immediacy,
- performance,
- novelty,
- and perpetual availability.  

The individual increasingly experiences silence, slowness, or invisibility as:

- irrelevance,
- social disappearance,
- stagnation,
- or personal diminishment.  

This creates environments where:

> visibility begins replacing grounded continuity.  

---

## 🧿 Distinctive Environmental Pressures  

Common contributing conditions include:

- infinite feeds,
- virality architectures,
- engagement metrics,
- influencer economies,
- algorithmic novelty prioritisation,
- and social performance cultures.  

These systems often collapse the boundary between:

- selfhood,
- audience expectation,
- and platform optimisation.  

The result is an ecology where:

> perpetual stimulation becomes normalised as emotional baseline.

## 🌱 Recovery Orientation  

Recovery within sanguine distempers often depends upon restoring:

- attentional continuity,
- private creativity,
- slowness,
- non-performative presence,
- and tolerance for reduced visibility.  

The goal is not withdrawal from expression or joy, but recovery of:

- grounded agency,
- sustainable attention,
- and selfhood beyond continuous audience feedback.
