# 💛 Choleric Distempers  
**First created:** 2026-05-12 | **Last updated:** 2026-05-21  
*Escalatory informational states associated with outrage dependency, adversarial identity formation, factional conflict, and compulsive moral combat.*  

---

## 🛰️ Orientation  

Choleric distempers emerge when conflict-oriented or justice-driven tendencies become locked into perpetual cycles of outrage, confrontation, and antagonistic reinforcement.  

These states are often characterised by:

- compulsive argumentation,
- adversarial identity consolidation,
- escalating hostility,
- purity enforcement,
- and emotional dependency upon conflict intensity itself.  

Unlike principled disagreement or ethical conviction, choleric distempers involve the progressive erosion of proportionality, ambiguity tolerance, and recovery capacity.  

Conflict ceases to function as:

- situational response,
- strategic intervention,
- or moral boundary-setting,  

and instead becomes a continuous behavioural atmosphere.  

Platform systems frequently intensify these dynamics by rewarding:

- emotional intensity,
- performative certainty,
- enemy construction,
- factional mobilisation,
- and visibility through escalation.

## 🔥 Common Dynamics  

Choleric distempers often emerge from adaptive capacities such as:

- protective instincts,
- political commitment,
- ethical seriousness,
- leadership drive,
- and willingness to confront harm.  

Destabilisation occurs when informational systems continuously reward emotional escalation over strategic effectiveness or relational continuity.  

Conflict itself becomes psychologically regulating.  

The individual increasingly experiences disengagement as:

- surrender,
- cowardice,
- complicity,
- or moral contamination.  

This creates environments where:

> intensity becomes mistaken for integrity.  

---

## 🧿 Distinctive Environmental Pressures  

Contributing conditions often include:

- outrage-driven engagement systems,
- polarised platform architectures,
- factional media ecosystems,
- callout visibility incentives,
- viral humiliation economies,
- and algorithmic conflict amplification.  

These environments frequently reward rapid escalation while punishing reflection, pacing, or uncertainty.  

The result is an ecology where:

> antagonism becomes socially profitable.

## 🌱 Recovery Orientation  

Recovery within choleric distempers usually depends upon restoring:

- proportionality,
- pacing,
- relational differentiation,
- strategic discernment,
- and nervous system de-escalation.  

The goal is not passivity or political disengagement, but recovery of:

- intentionality,
- flexibility,
