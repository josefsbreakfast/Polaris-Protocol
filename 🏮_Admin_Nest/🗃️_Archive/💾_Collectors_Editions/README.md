# 💾 Collectors Editions  
**First created:** 2026-05-25 | **Last updated:** 2026-05-25  
*Curated historical materials, preserved archive states, and culturally interesting artefacts retained for continuity, readability, and archival charm within the Polaris Protocol.*

---

## 🧭 Orientation  

Collectors Editions contains preserved Polaris materials considered:
- historically interesting,
- culturally revealing,
- structurally significant,
- unusually entertaining,
- or valuable as artefacts of archive evolution.

Unlike routine deprecated paperwork routed into Room 101, materials here are intentionally curated and preserved.

Some entries may be:
- lightly updated for readability,
- reorganised for easier navigation,
- or tagged to indicate historical age or development phase.

---

## 💾 What Lives Here  

Typical contents may include:
- historically notable drafts,
- memorable governance artefacts,
- culturally revealing archive states,
- unusually illustrative systems experiments,
- humorous administrative remnants,
- or preserved snapshots from earlier Polaris phases.

These materials are not always canonical.

Sometimes they remain because they:
- reveal how Polaris evolved,
- capture useful framing or humour,
- preserve historically important moments,
- or simply deserve to survive as part of the archive’s cultural memory.

## ⚠️ Governance Note  

Collectors Editions is archival rather than canonical.

Materials here:
- may not reflect current governance standards,
- may preserve outdated structure,
