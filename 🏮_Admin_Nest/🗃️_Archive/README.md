## 🧭 Orientation  

The Archive contains materials no longer considered part of the active canonical Polaris structure.

This folder exists to preserve:
- historical development pathways,
- deprecated governance systems,
- retired scaffolding,
- experimental structures,
- and earlier archive states.

Retention does not necessarily imply endorsement or active use.

Some materials remain valuable primarily as:
- reconstruction reference,
- historical context,
- governance archaeology,
- or because they are simply too interesting to disappear entirely.
