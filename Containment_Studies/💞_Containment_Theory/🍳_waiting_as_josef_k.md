## Core Theory: Waiting-as-Containment

Waiting-as-containment has five defining properties:

1. **No Stated Trigger**  
   The subject is never told what initiated the process.

2. **No Stated Endpoint**  
   Time passes without criteria for completion.

3. **No Legible Authority**  
   Decisions appear to occur elsewhere, without named agents.

4. **Silence as Procedure**  
   Non-response is not absence of action; it *is* the action.

5. **Endurance Recast as Responsibility**  
   Stability, restraint, and patience are demanded of the subject, while the system remains opaque.

Waiting is not a gap between events.  
**Waiting is the event.**
